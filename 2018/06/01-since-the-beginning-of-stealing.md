Since The Beginning Of Stealing
-------------------------------
And a imperalist necklace's ice will stand you. When you tread like tree trod by the lightning.  
I was without doubt the woman oyster  
there in the ironous modern office.  
When it looked me with its electric serenity eyes  
it had neither leg nor lip  
but cork essences on its sides.  
Felicity is gone, the subject has woke.
