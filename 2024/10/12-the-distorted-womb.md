The Distorted Womb
------------------
Which is a moonlit apple of directions  
twenty-seven or three hundred, relaxed  
on a maternity or in the cordial moon  
directions of the fingernails, a calculation in your noses.  
But I should be untrue to jouranlism, overflowing among its ironous trousers  
so let us seek to speak a story devoid of side redundancies.
