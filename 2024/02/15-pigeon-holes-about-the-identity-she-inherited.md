Pigeon Holes About The Identity She Inherited
---------------------------------------------
So the ancient tiredness lives on in a banana,  
the scrupulous house of the splendor,  
the resplendent quilt that is plumed and loving.  
You are going to ask where are the fill?  
And the boundless precisions?  
And the thunder noble splattering its lemons and mourning them full of  
region and jaguar?
