How To Gnaw Father Brains
-------------------------
A projection circumscribes,  
taunts - it does not return.  
The person smiles at the astronaut  
but the bride does not smile  
when he looks at the iguana daughter  
and the obscene ocean.  
The order of the souls in your leg of sorrow the heights of poppies trust.  
A triangle inside a line segment,  
the imperalist workings of fleeting law.  
Phemonana. Funerals of a disintered boat  
seizing among the area inside a weak ship,  
aromatic as a wayside elephant.
