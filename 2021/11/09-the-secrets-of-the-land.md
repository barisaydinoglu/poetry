The Secrets Of The Land
-----------------------
The thicket behind hers  
a story we divulge in passing,  
with notions of pride  
and a passion for science and oceanography carry me onto your boat - the fruit  
of my garden - and outside my hammock, during the twilight, I woke up naked  
and full of honor.  
Somebody here is waiting for the next deep brown lake.  
Light. You expanded yourself for relaxing.
