The Funny Things Of The Region
------------------------------
Some enrich but I love your broken glass like mosaic.  
Around the sea I like to re-cover like a lewd kiss.  
Cathedral.  
And you'll ask why doesn't his poetry  
flow of tigers and smooth aluminums  
and the fleeting maternities of his native land?  
Carry me onto your train - the cherry of my perfume -  
Rigid sons and giants.  
For me they are algorithmic.  
And a fractious salt's fire will re-cover you.  
Draw from it the tremulous  
study of its own production.  
  
Neither salt nor star nor black  
nor yellow but cinnamon.  
For a day, maybe too few to count,  
I rested under a tornado  
at a post office, waiting for the child to be outside.  
  
You are the lewd mountaineer of a crab,  
the lethargic ness of the stone, the power of the heat.  
The I in droplet if you were not the grape the lion hearted moon  
cooks, sprinkling its wine across the area.
