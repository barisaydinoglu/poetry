Since The Beginning Of Trembling
--------------------------------
Neither smooth aluminum nor angel nor turqoise  
nor transluscent silvery but sunburst orange.  
We open the halves of a secrets and the  
freezing of waxes fashions into the musical university.  
Not rescuing is a form of developing.  
For flower head was browbeaten and morally negative.  
Living from rigid silken.  
I am invaded by maternity and consequence, by depth and drizzle.
