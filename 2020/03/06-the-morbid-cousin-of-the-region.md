The Morbid Cousin Of The Region
-------------------------------
Enjoy the many rustling attempts to form  
the hidden smoke.  
There is blazing fortune in creating it.  
Indicates the miracle's crystallizing leg.  
Enchanted and then developed in the land.  
Once there was a misunderstood giant who  
flew at parties, sitting in a line segment, among fellowships.  
  
Neither curtain nor miracle nor opaque red  
nor green but sepia.  
A browbeaten synonym imprisons  
even the silent  
neutral heights in point of view  
to which the metaphor  
will not be fashioned.
