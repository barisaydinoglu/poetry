How To Dismantle Gentleman Hearts
---------------------------------
The careless old warrior's medal that entertains in your stone.  
Behind the wayside heights of smothered goblet.  
Some drink but I dedicate your rusted nail like light.  
But the flesh trod the memory.  
I do not smear in the heights of rotten complaint.  
The thorn tree chirps on its muzzled mare  
building transparent momenta over the field.  
In the first reel, the musical giant  
is undulated by a bride. In the second  
scene he returns, to trust and to live.
